## Known issues / to investigate

- **Cross-source numbering-scheme mismatch.** MangaDex lists sub-chapters as Vol.1 Ch.1.1/1.2 while WeebCentral lists whole chapters 1..14 for the same content (e.g. "At Home with a Girl in Her Cute Pajamas"). These are distinct `Number`s so they coexist as extra rows. **Investigated 2026-07: no safe automatic merge exists.** Collapsing x.1/x.2 → x is indistinguishable from legitimate decimal extras (10.5 omake chapters), the parts-per-chapter count isn't knowable from metadata, and the rows represent different physical release units — merging would break the one-chapter-row-one-file invariant that downloads/linking rely on. Workaround: disable one of the source mappings on the affected series — the clash detector (see Done) now flags affected series automatically.
